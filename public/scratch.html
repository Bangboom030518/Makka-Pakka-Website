Makka Pakka - Scratch Projects

Scratch Projects

Makka Pakka's Rock Ai

Do you love rocks? Are you in the Makka Pakka fan club? You'll love impersonating him by stacking these rocks. Try and arange them into piles of three, and press space or the green flag to shuffle them. Hours of fun!


Makka Pakka's Trumpet

This project is a homage to one of makka pakka's most iconic assets: his trumpet. Press it to make that incredible sound. Be warned though, it may make some slightly odd noises instead. I promise the trumpet isn't just a sideways traffic cone.


Makka Pakka's Maze

This is the new among us for makka pakka fans, coming soon to play station, Windows 10, Xbox and Nintendo Switch. Walk through the door to start, using the arrow keys to move. Collect the rocks and avoid hitting the banks. Best experienced on a gaming pc.


Makka Pakka's Falling Rocks

Another graphically intense masterpiece. Simply press play and feel your frame rate drop quicker than your sanity. Use the arrow keys to move makka pakka from side to side, helping him go shopping. make sure not to let any fall to the ground or he will have to put you in detention.
